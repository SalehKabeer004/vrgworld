About us

A digital payments platform for the many.

  • VRG is a mobile financial services platform for banking customers and the unbanked.
  • Designed from ground up for lowest acquisition and transaction costs
  • Allows unparalleled interoperability in the fragmented payments ecosystem of Pakistan
  • Enables banks to offer their customers a seamless mobile experience that transforms customer engagement
  • Powerful systems integration capability means we can plug into any heterogeneous & legacy IT environment
  • Built with consultation, alignment and buy-in of both PTA and State Bank
  • Backed by the financial stability of the Pathfinder group
